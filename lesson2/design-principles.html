White Space and Clean Design

Brandon Sanderson

Brandon Sanderson

The website for Brandon Sanderson shows good white space or in this case black space by giving nice boarders around each of its elements. However, as you scroll down on the phone sized website it starts to lose this white space.

PARC: Alignment

D&D Beyond

D&D Beyond

The D&D Beyond website align up these buttons that lead to different functions of the website. While these are not straight lines they are over all straight and aligning them adds a clean view of the site.

PARC: Contrast

Lego

Lego

The website for lego demonstartes good contrast. They have a mostly white background with back text. There images also have a white background which draws out the colors. Lastly they have splashes of yellow or orange with black text on them.
