Site Name and Domain

The name of the website is: Berk's Chamber of Commerce, and its domain is: berkschamberofcommerce.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to centralize and standerize the trade of Berk with its new aquasision of dragons. While dragons in the past have been a detrament to trade, with their taming it opens up brand new oppertunites for new wealth. As such this site plans to list possible ocupations and goods that should stay within Berk and several that we can export out of Berk.
