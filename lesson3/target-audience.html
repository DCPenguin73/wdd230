Target Audience

The target audience is citizens of Berk looking to make money. With a secondary audience of those looking to come to Berk to trade or sell their goods.

Personas

Biorn Larson

Biorn is a forty five year old male looking to start a bussiness selling dragon carved sculptures. His motive for using the site is to figure out if he can export these works of art and how to best start his bussiness.

Greta Hofferson

Greta is a twenty one year old female looking to come to Berk to expeiance tamed dragons. Her motive for using the site is to find when the next festival is and what goods she could buy while she is there.

Scenarios

  1. How can I start a bussiness and what are some good ideas for a bussiness?
  2. What goods can be exported from Berk?
  3. How can I promote my bussiness?
  4. What shops are in Berk?
  5. When is the next market festival?
